The New Neighbours

Meet your new neighbours (They're not at all creepy)

You're new to the neighbourhood and needed to borrow a cup of sugar.

After walking through the gate of the neighbour's property and up their drive you come to the front door of the house.


The door opens into the hall; the butler is standing there, you explain your visit and he grunts and points North...



You find your self in the hall, the only light is coming from the doorway at your back, you peer into the darkness
